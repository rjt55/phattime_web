Boar Luring 101

In order to successfully lure and kill a boar for its sustenance we must first have roughly 6 available villagers.

While 5 of the villagers are slaughtering lamb by your town center, take one of them to a known boar location.

Shoot two arrows at the boar and run your villager the hell back to your town center, less you allow it to be mauled to death.

As the boar approaches the town center where the other villagers are, they should stop what they are doing and fire arrows and kill the boar.

You may need to garrison the villager used to lure the board, less again, it may be mauled the death.

Once the boar is killed ungarrison the villager and have all of them pick the boar clean for its juicy meat.
